Grounded, not black-box

  • Derived metrics are computed with a transparent, reviewable formula set — never a black-box model prediction.
  • Every fact and filing links back to its source on SEC EDGAR.
  • The optional AI leadership analyzer extracts initiatives and quotes strictly from a company's own filing excerpts, verbatim only — off by default, with no API call unless configured.
  • The stakeholder orientation index is a capital-allocation heuristic, not a rating, score, or judgment of any named person.
  • Nothing on this site is investment, financial, HR, or legal advice.
